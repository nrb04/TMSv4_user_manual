\subsubsection{ SMS Graphic View}

In this section, users can view the \textbf{ SMS Traffic} data in a graphical format. The bar chart displays the traffic volume for different categories such as \textbf{ON-NET}, \textbf{OFF-NET (IN)}, \textbf{OFF-NET (OUT)}, \textbf{INTNL (IN)}, and \textbf{INTNL (OUT)}, each represented by different colors. The chart is interactive, allowing users to visually compare the data over a specific time period, such as daily traffic data for each operator.

By selecting different filters or adjusting the time range, users can analyze the traffic trends for each category, allowing for deeper insights into the network's performance.

\subsubsection{ SMS Statistics View}

In this section, users can view the \textbf{ SMS Traffic Statistics} in a visual format. The statistics display aggregated data, including the total traffic, operators involved, the peak value (single point), and the number of periods. The graph in the center shows the distribution of the traffic among different operators.

For example, as shown in the image, users can see the breakdown of the traffic for operators such as \textbf{Banglalink}, \textbf{Grameenphone}, \textbf{Robi Axiata}, and \textbf{Teletalk} in a pie chart format. The statistics on the right side display the \textbf{total traffic} aggregated over the selected period, as well as other important metrics like the \textbf{peak} traffic value and the \textbf{number of periods} for which the data is being viewed.
